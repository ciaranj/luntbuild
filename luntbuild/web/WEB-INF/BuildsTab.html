This page shows build information for all projects and all views. Status of a schedule and build is denoted in icon: GREEN icon stands for success, The animation gear icon stands for building, and RED icon stands for failed. Schedule status is different from build status, and it means whether or not the schedule has been successfully triggered. Trigger of the schedule may or may not generate a new build, depends on current build strategy and repository changes. The system log contains detail information about the execution of a schedule.
